SiU-Bike Time

Hey guys! We are SiU-Bike (Smile interactive U-Bike) team from the department of Computer Science of National Cheng Chi University in Taiwan, through the integration of IoT sensing devices, including MonkeyLectric Bike Light, Arduino, BLE4.0, etc. With MonkeyLectric Bike Light’s assistance, our team implemented a fantastic and smart bike (SiU-Bike) that can change its LED pattern on wheel depending on environmental detections.

SiU-Bike is similar to a digital advertising bike in the future, and we hope that emerging business value extends to other aspects.With the goal of pursuing the academic award of research and creation, I and my partner will hold a exhibition—“DCT (Digital Content Technology)進行式.”

We will promote our eight-month creation, SiU-Bike, to the public, media and even the U-Bike system of Taipei City Government, realising the vision of Smart City.

See you guys in “DCT (Digital Content Technology)進行式”
at Songshan Cultural and Creative Park during 2015/10/17 (Sat.)~10/22 (Thu.).
